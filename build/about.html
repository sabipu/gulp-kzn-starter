About KZN.

KZN is a Perth based boutique consulting firm with extensive AWS and IT development experience. Our mission is to simplify the lives of our customers. KZN Group aim to be different. Our unique company culture allows us to attract, engage, motivate and retain the best talent, so that we can deliver the maximum value to the businesses we partner with.

Our team has over 140 years of collective experience. Our 13 staff have an average of more than 15 years honing their skills and gaining qualifications in IT strategy, planning, design, execution, engineering and operations.

Founded. 2015 Team size. 15 Location. Perth, Western Australia Specialisations. AWS, DevOps, data engineering,consulting, Agile
